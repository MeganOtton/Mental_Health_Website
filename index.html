What is Mental Health?

Mental health is our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices. Maintaining a good mental health will affect how you perceive and handle everyday life.

To learn how to improve your mental health look down below!

Mental Health Information

What to watch out for:
  • Persistent sadness or depression
  • Excessive fears or worries
  • Extreme mood changes
  • Withdrawal from friends and activities
  • Significant tiredness or low energy
